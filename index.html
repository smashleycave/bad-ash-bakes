About Bad Ash

Hi I'm Ashley!!!

My baking career began when I got my first Easy Bake Oven. How can a girl resist churning out cookies, cakes, brownies with the help of a 65 watt lightbulb?

As I got older, baking became my favorite past time. While working in IT, I became an avid stress baker. When life is tough, the tough get baking!! Soon, I was making so may pastries that i began began to share my bakes with my friends and family. Everyone loved them and swore that I could make a living doing something that I loved. So decided to turn my passion into a business!
